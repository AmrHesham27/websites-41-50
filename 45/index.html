Questions

Frequently asked

Common questions about engagements, IP, and timelines.

How long does a typical engagement take?
Pilot engagements typically run 8–12 weeks. Full product deliveries vary by scope — we provide a timeline in the proposal.
Do you provide ongoing support?
Yes — we offer runbook hand-off, SRE support, and managed options depending on your needs.
Who owns the IP?
IP terms are agreed in the contract. We commonly transfer deliverables while preserving libraries and tooling as agreed.
How do you manage model governance?
We include testing, bias checks, and model cards — we can adapt governance to your compliance needs.
