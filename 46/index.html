Frequently asked

Answers to common questions about process, timelines, and IP.

How long does an engagement typically take?

We run discovery sprints of 2–4 weeks followed by iterative delivery. Full platform engagements vary but are typically 3–9 months.

Who owns the models and IP?

We work under clear contractual terms — most clients retain IP while we provide engineering and support under agreed licenses or transfer terms.

Do you support on-premise or air-gapped deployments?

Yes. We design deployment options to meet security and compliance requirements, including on-prem or isolated environments.
